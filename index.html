Rise of AI

The Age of Mass Intelligence

Rise of AI: The Age of Mass Intelligence is a journey through the past, present, and future of intelligence. Blending science, philosophy, and imagination, it asks how humanity’s oldest sparks — fire, language, story — connect to today’s networks, learning machines, and beyond.

At its heart is a reflection: intelligence is not just something we build, but something we share. Each chapter explores how our tools reshape us, how our connections form new constellations of thought, and how we might carry this lantern forward with care.

More than a history, this is a meditation on responsibility and possibility — a book about resonance, stewardship, and the ongoing question the universe still asks through us: what am I?
